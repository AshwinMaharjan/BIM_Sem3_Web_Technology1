Popular Places in Nepal

Place 1 Pashupatinath: One of the most sacred and ancient places to visit in Nepal, Pashupathinath Temple is located on the banks of the divine Baramati River.

  Place 2 Nagarkot: It is a paradise for hikers or adventure lovers as well as serenity seekers.

Place 3 Chandragiri: Known for its cable car ride, Chandragiri Hills offer captivated views of the Himalayan ranges.
